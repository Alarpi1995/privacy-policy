Privacy Policy

SmartChatBot - Intelligent Social Media Management Platform

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartChatBot. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile picture when you sign in
  • Social Media Data: When connecting accounts (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp), we access pages/accounts and messaging permissions
  • Conversation Data: Messages exchanged with your customers through connected platforms
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our application
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device identifiers

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing messaging and customer communication services
  • Creating, scheduling, and publishing social media posts
  • Delivering AI-powered automated responses
  • Analyzing engagement metrics and providing insights
  • Improving and developing our services
  • Sending important notifications about your account

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Meta) to execute requested services
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the application
  • When required to comply with laws or legal orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Secure storage of access tokens and credentials
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to sensitive data on a need-to-know basis
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Disconnect: Unlink social media accounts at any time
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze usage patterns. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

8. Facebook/Meta Permissions

When connecting a Facebook account, we request the following permissions:

  • pages_show_list - View your list of pages
  • pages_messaging - Manage messages on your pages
  • pages_manage_metadata - Manage page metadata
  • pages_read_engagement - Read engagement statistics
  • pages_manage_posts - Create and manage posts

You can revoke these permissions at any time from your Facebook settings.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, unless retention is required by law.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or an in-app notification.
